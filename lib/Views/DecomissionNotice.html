Attention Investor Map users

The Investor Map will be de-commissioned on September 28th. Austrade has been working with Data 61 and Geoscience Australia to ensure that the majority of data sets surfaced by the Investor Map including land tenure and use, infrastructure, climate, resource location and availability, and others, will continue to be available and accessible for users on other digital platforms.

For further details visit www.austrade.gov.au/International/Invest/Resources/Datasets.
